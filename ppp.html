Dynamo Metrics
&
City of Jackson


In this extra-curriculum project, I worked with four other students majoring information and engineering to deliver user experience consultancy for a data analytics and visualization company.



This is an ongoing project.

Problem Statement

As a data analytics and visualization program, Dynamo Metrics has been working with city governments to address social and economical problems by leveraging the power of data. For their upcoming project with the City of Jackson in Michigan, the company is in need of insights from UX designers and data scientists to help deliver optimal solution.


Role

Working in a team of five, I participated in all research and design process with two other student designers and two student data scientists, while being in charge of project management for the team.


User Research

To better understand the workflow of the end users, we have conducted four interviews at the City of Jackson with potential users in the government. Prior to the interview, the team worked together on a semi-structured interview protocol with an aim to elicit specific detailsregarding the interviewee's background, job responsibilities. By asking the interviewees to walk us through their job tasks over the day with live demonstrations, we have garnered good understanding of their current workflow and tools, as well as challenges at work.



After the interview, the UX team interpreted the data and organized the notes into a structured organization, including:


  • Daily tasks
  • Duties
  • Issues regarding workflow
  • Issues regarding software tools
  • Data types in need


Meanwhile, each one of the designers was also in charge of making a persona to provide vivid represenations of end users for our client.
