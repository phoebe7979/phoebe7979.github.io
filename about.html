Hello! I am Yi-Chun, an aspiring UX professional based in Michigan.

Working on UX projects, I incorporate my past research skills and knowledge in communication mechanisms into the process, while leveraging my business experience, project management capabilities, and communication expertise to ideate, execute, and deliver streamlined designs.
